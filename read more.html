About Me:

I am a 34-year-old educator that is appointed by the SGB of Uitsig High School. I am a hard-working person that embrace life with great enthusiasm. I have a lot of passion for my calling as well as the subjects in which I received training. I believe that I will be of great value to any school and contribute positively to the lives of learners and colleagues alike.

I matriculated in 2004 and started with my BEd degree at the University of Pretoria. Unfortunately, I had to put my studies on hold at the end of my second year, I did however not give up on my goals and was given an opportunity to continue with my degree at the University of South Africa in 2012. I was offered an opportunity by the SGB of Amandasig Secondary School to teach Afrikaans in 2014, after which I graduated in 2015.

I believe that Amandasig Secondary School and Uitsig High School respectively afforded me the opportunity to develop as a teacher and the person I am today. I do however have a lot more to offer and willing to spread my wings and avail myself to new experiences. I recently also started an online course on web development with Udemy.
